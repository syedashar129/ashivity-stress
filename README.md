# ashivity-stress

Real-time stress management and monitoring for Apple Watch. Track your stress levels, receive intelligent notifications, and take control of your wellness with ashivity-stress.

## 📱 Overview

ashivity-stress is an Apple Watch companion app designed to help you monitor and manage stress in real-time. Similar to the Livity app, it provides comprehensive stress metrics, actionable insights, and timely notifications to help you maintain emotional well-being throughout the day.

### Key Features

- **Real-Time Stress Monitoring** – Continuous tracking of your stress levels on your wrist
- **Smart Notifications** – Receive intelligent alerts when stress levels spike
- **Metrics Dashboard** – Visualize stress patterns and trends over time
- **Health Integration** – Seamless integration with Apple Health data
- **Wrist Feedback** – Haptic feedback and visual cues for stress alerts
- **Wellness Insights** – Personalized recommendations based on your stress data

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Installation

1. Clone the repository
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/syedashar129/ashivity-stress.git
   cd ashivity-stress
   ```

2. Open in Xcode
   ```bash
   open ashivity-stress.xcodeproj
   ```

3. Select the ashivity-stress Watch target and build

### Building from Source

- Xcode 13.0 or later
- Swift 5.5 or later

## 📖 Usage

1. **Launch the App** – Open ashivity-stress on your Apple Watch
2. **Check Your Status** – View current stress level on the main screen
3. **Log Events** – Tap to log events that might affect your stress
4. **Review Notifications** – Respond to and dismiss stress alerts
5. **Track Progress** – Check your trends and historical data

## 🎓 How It Works

The app uses sensor data from your Apple Watch combined with user input to calculate stress levels. When significant stress is detected, you'll receive a notification with options to:

- Log what triggered the stress
- Note your response
- View helpful calming techniques

All data is securely stored and integrated with Apple Health for a holistic view of your wellness.
